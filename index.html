About SGPA and Percentage

SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average) is a grading system used in many universities to measure a student's academic performance in a semester.

The most common formula to convert SGPA to Percentage is: Percentage = (SGPA × 10) - 7.5

To convert Percentage to SGPA: SGPA = (Percentage + 7.5) / 10

Note: Some universities may use different conversion formulas. Please check with your institution for the exact formula.
